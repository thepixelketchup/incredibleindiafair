OUR STORY

A journey from Amsterdam to the heart of Indian craft.

For over a decade, Divya and Shreya have called the Netherlands home.

Living away from India, they often found themselves carrying pieces of it with them — a handwoven textile, a small handcrafted gift, something that held a story. And every time they gifted these to their Dutch friends, the reaction was always the same: curiosity, appreciation, and joy.

A Simple Realization

It was in those moments that something quietly began to take shape. A simple thought: Why is something so rich, so meaningful, still so distant?

Over the years, that thought grew into a deeper realization. Across the world, people were beginning to value handmade, slow, and meaningful products from India — yet the very communities behind these crafts were slowly fading into the background.

Because the truth is, craft is not just a product. It is memory. It is identity. It is generations of knowledge passed down by hands. And without support, without visibility, without connection, it risks being lost.

THE MISSION

What started as small personal moments turned into a larger purpose: to create a bridge between India and Europe.

To bring not just products, but stories.
Not just commerce, but connection.

After years of exploring trade fairs across India and Europe, learning what is missing, Divya and Shreya decided to bring it all together.

Incredible India Fair was born from that journey.

A space where craft meets culture, where artisans find new audiences, and where people don’t just shop — they experience. Because this is not just about showcasing India. It is about keeping its stories alive.
